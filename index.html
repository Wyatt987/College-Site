Did You Know?

  • IU Bloomington has over 750 student organizations!
  • The campus covers nearly 2,000 acres of land.
  • IU's library system is among the largest in the nation.

Sports

Indiana University is home to the Hoosiers, with 24 varsity sports teams competing in the NCAA Division I Big Ten Conference. The men's basketball team has won five national championships, and IU's athletic tradition is one of the strongest in the country.

Notable Alumni

IU has produced many distinguished alumni, including Mark Cuban (entrepreneur and owner of the Dallas Mavericks), Suzanne Collins (author of The Hunger Games), and Hoagy Carmichael (composer of \"Stardust\").

Tuition

For the 2025-2026 academic year, in-state undergraduate tuition and fees are approximately $11,500 per year, while out-of-state students pay about $40,000. Financial aid and scholarships are available to help make IU affordable for all students.
